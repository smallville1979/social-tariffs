⚡ Energy

Cut Your
Energy Bills

Most people on benefits don't know half of this exists. All of it is yours by right.

🌡️

Warm Home Discount — £150

On Pension Credit, UC, or Income Support? You may get £150 off your electricity bill. Check with your supplier — some get it automatically, others need to apply before the deadline.

💷 £150 off your bill
❄️

Cold Weather Payment

Automatic £25 payment when local temperature is at or forecast to be 0°C or below for 7 consecutive days. If you're on qualifying benefits, you should receive it automatically.

🌨️ Automatic if eligible
🔋

Emergency Credit

On a prepayment meter and can't top up? Call your supplier — every major UK supplier must provide emergency credit immediately. You repay gradually when you next top up.

📞 Call your supplier now
🏠

ECO4 — Free Insulation & Boilers

Free home insulation, boiler upgrades, and energy improvements for people on qualifying benefits. Saves hundreds per year. Check eligibility at gov.uk or through your supplier.

🆓 Free home upgrades
🎟️

CAB Fuel Vouchers

Citizens Advice can provide fuel vouchers for gas and electric — up to 3 times in any 6-month period. Almost nobody knows this. Walk in and ask specifically for fuel vouchers.

3x per 6 months — free
